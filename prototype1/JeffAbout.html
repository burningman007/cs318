Jeff and Friend's Goal

About Jeff

Jeff is a “Man of Mystery”, shrouded in shadow and anonymity. He is known by many names, but “Jeff” is most often spoken when people refer to him. Jeff is a friend first and an artist second. He is a builder, creator, painter, drawer, and photographer, but more importantly he is comrade to anyone that is in need of it. Jeff has many friends but enjoys his privacy as well. Because of this privacy Jeff is known by many but many do not know his true identity.

About Jeff's Friends

Jeff has many friends, some of which know his identity while others don't. In order to be a part of "Jeff's friends" you must believe in a similar way of life to that of Jeff. If you want to become one of Jeffs Friends go to our contact page and fill in the information and well do our best to get you in the group of Jeff's Friends.
