What is Jeff and Friends Make Things?

Jeff and friends is a site specially made for a man or woman (Who Knows) named Jeff. Jeff is a student at Eau Claire and and loves to make things. He is not an art major but very much likes anything that is artistic. Jeff wishes for the whole world to appreciate art in the way that he does and wants to encurage anyone, regardless of job title or college major to pursue any sort of artistry that they have ever wanted to do. Jeff and Friends Make Things is a place to display these pieces of artwork. No matter who you are, if you feel like posting your piece on this sight, feel free to contact us and we will do our best to get it up for all the world to see.

Jeff and Friend's Goal

Jeff and Friend's Goal is to get more people involved in art, regardless of if they consider themselves an artist or not. None of the works we have on this site are for sale and are owned only by the artists that created them, and are not made to be sold or put into circulation other than on this site.

About Jeff

Jeff is the Pen name of a Man or Woman. All that can be said about this mythical creater is that he is not a art student. Jeff believes whole hardedly that you do not need to be a art major to appreciate and create art and hopes people can come to this site and check out some cool art.

About Jeff's Friends

Jeff has many friends, some of which know his identity while others don't. In order to be a part of "Jeff's friends" you must believe in a similar way of life to that of Jeff. If you want to become one of Jeffs Friends go to our contact page and fill in the information and well do our best to get you in the group of Jeff's Friends.
